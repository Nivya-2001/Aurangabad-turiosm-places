H2O Water Park Rides :


The park has many exciting rides for children and adults including-
1) Tube rides
2) 40 feet drop slides
3) Cyclone rides
4) Frog and crocodile slides for children
5) Two rain dance floors with a separate section for families.
6) The food corner which serves lip-smacking food including drinks and cocktails.


Dress Code :


1. Appropriate swimwear must be worn when using the park’s attractions and if wearing inappropriate attire, you might be asked to change or leave the premises. Swimwear and casual clothes are available on rent. 2. Clothing should not indecently expose parts of the body, be transparent or display obscene or offensive pictures and slogans. 3. Regular diapers are not permitted in any pool or attraction. 4. Inappropriate swimwear includes underwear, transparent bathing suits, sarees, jewellery, swimsuits with buckles, chains or with metal.


Do's and Dont's :


    Do’s :
  • Follow the directions of staff and lifeguards at all times.
  • Make sure you are medically fit for any slides or attractions.
  • Wearing sunglasses and prescription lenses are not allowed when riding.
  • Outside food and beverages are allowed only around Canteen Area.
  • Read the signs indicating height restrictions before entering any slides or attractions.
    Dont’s :
  • Dangerous articles including sharp objects, explosives, fireworks or chemicals are not allowed.
  • Alcoholic beverages and drugs are prohibited. Smoking is allowed in designated areas only.
  • Regular diapers for children not allowed during swimming.
  • Do not run, dive, stand, kneel, rotate or stop in the slides.
  • Pregnant women or people with heart conditions or back trouble should avoid riding the slides.
